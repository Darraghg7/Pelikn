Login, dashboard, and your first 15 minutes in Pelikn
💡
How to access Pelikn
Your venue has a unique URL: pelikn.app/v/your-venue-name. Staff log in with a PIN shown on the login screen. Managers log in at pelikn.app/login with email + password. Add Pelikn to your phone's home screen for app-like access — it works offline too.

🔐 Two types of login

📱 Staff PIN Login
Staff tap their name on the venue login screen, enter their 4–6 digit PIN, and they're in. No email needed. PINs are set up by the manager in Settings → Staff.
🔑 Manager Email Login
Owners and managers sign in at pelikn.app/login with email + password. This gives access to all management features, exports, and settings — not available on staff accounts.

Food Safety & Compliance
8 digital log books replacing paper — all EHO-ready

🌡️ UK Temperature Quick Reference

Check Type Safe Range Frequency Action if Failed
Fridge / Chilled 0°C – 5°C AM & PM daily Log reason, monitor, escalate if persistent
Freezer −18°C or below AM & PM daily Check seals, transfer product if needed
Cooking / Reheating 75°C or above Every batch Return to heat, do not serve until passed
Hot Holding 63°C or above AM & PM service Reheat or discard within 2 hours
Delivery — Chilled 0°C – 5°C Every delivery Reject delivery, notify supplier
Delivery — Frozen −25°C – −18°C Every delivery Reject delivery, notify supplier
Cooling (start) From 70°C → ≤8°C Every batch cooled Must reach ≤8°C within 90 minutes

✅ Are you EHO-Ready? — Daily checklist for managers

Fridge AM & PM temps logged today
Opening checklist completed
Cooking temps recorded for every batch
Hot holding AM check done
All deliveries logged and accepted/rejected
Fitness to work declared by all staff
No overdue cleaning tasks
Allergen info up to date
Corrective actions logged for any issues
Closing checklist completed at end of day
🏆
EHO inspections — what they look for
Inspectors want to see consistent, dated records over time — not perfect scores. A venue with 6 months of continuous, properly-explained logs (even with some flagged readings) will always score better than one with gaps. Pelikn's continuous log books are exactly what they want to see.
