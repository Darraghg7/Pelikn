# Pelikn — Marketing Plan

_Updated: May 2026_

---

## Strategy in One Line

**Compliance gets them in. Team management makes them switch to Pro.**

The job of marketing is to put Pelikn in front of a venue owner at the exact moment compliance stress is highest — failed EHO inspection, upcoming audit, growing team, opening a second site — then convert them through a trial and into a paying Pro subscriber.

---

## Messaging Hierarchy

Every touchpoint should follow this order. Don't jump to team management before compliance lands.

```
1. HOOK     → EHO compliance, sorted. Digital logs, audit-ready reports, one app.
2. EXPAND   → And when you're ready, manage your whole team from the same place.
3. CLOSE    → £25/month per venue. No per-user fees. No contracts. Cancel anytime.
```

**Primary headline:** *"Built for Hospitality. Built for Compliance."*

**Supporting lines:**
- *"Replace paper logs with digital records your EHO inspector will love."*
- *"Add your rota, timesheets, and training records — and ditch three other apps."*
- *"Flat fee per venue. 20 staff or 2 — the price doesn't change."*

## Acquisition — Getting Found

### Priority 1: SEO & Content

Compliance is a search-intent goldmine. Venue owners google these problems at the exact moment they need Pelikn. Rank for them.

**Target keyword clusters:**

| Intent | Keywords |
|---|---|
| Compliance pain | "food safety app UK", "digital temperature log restaurant", "EHO inspection checklist", "HACCP plan template free" |
| Audit prep | "how to pass EHO inspection", "food hygiene rating 5 tips", "EHO audit ready checklist" |
| Competitor switch | "Trail app alternative", "Deputy alternative UK hospitality", "restaurant rota software UK" |
| Natasha's Law | "allergen management software UK", "natasha's law compliance", "allergen register restaurant" |
| Team management | "restaurant rota app UK", "hospitality timesheet software", "staff scheduling hospitality" |

**Content to create (in priority order):**

1. **"How to Pass Your EHO Inspection"** — long-form guide, downloadable PDF checklist. This is the highest-intent piece. Ranks for multiple keywords, captures email, introduces the product.

2. **"Free HACCP Plan Template for UK Restaurants"** — lead magnet. Downloadable Word/PDF. Captures email from venues doing compliance manually. Funnel them into a trial.

3. **"Natasha's Law: What Every UK Venue Needs to Do"** — evergreen compliance explainer. Demonstrates UK legal expertise. Converts venues worried about allergen compliance.

4. **"Trail App vs Pelikn: Which Is Right for Your Venue?"** — comparison page targeting Trail's existing (paying, compliance-educated) customer base. Honest, not aggressive. Show that Trail does compliance well but costs more and doesn't do scheduling.

5. **"The Real Cost of Deputy for a 20-Person Hospitality Team"** — calculator content targeting Deputy's per-user pricing pain. Lets operators run their own numbers.

6. **"How to Build a Fair Rota for Your Restaurant"** — operational guide targeting scheduling keywords. Naturally introduces AI rota builder.

### Priority 2: Paid Search

Run tightly scoped Google Ads on the highest-intent compliance terms. Small budget, high intent.

**Campaigns:**
- `[EHO compliance software]` — direct purchase intent
- `[food safety app restaurant UK]` — product awareness
- `[trail app alternative]` — competitor displacement (works well for priced-out Trail users)
- `[restaurant rota software UK]` — team management intent

**Ad copy frame:** Lead with compliance, trail with team management, close with price.
> *"EHO-Ready Compliance Records + AI Rota Builder — £25/month per venue. 7-day free trial."*

### Priority 3: Trade & Community

UK hospitality operators cluster in specific places. Be present there.

**Trade press:** The Caterer, Big Hospitality, Morning Advertiser, Propel. A contributed article ("How to protect your food hygiene rating in 2026") builds credibility and reaches decision-makers.

**Communities:**
- Facebook groups: "UK Restaurant Owners", "Hospitality Professionals UK", EHO/food safety consultant groups
- Reddit: r/KitchenConfidential, r/ChefLife
- LinkedIn: Target venue owners, GMs, and operations managers at 1–10 site groups

**Partnerships:**
- Food safety consultants and EHO prep consultants — they advise venues on compliance and could recommend Pelikn
- Hospitality accountants — they deal with payroll/timesheet headaches and could refer clients
- Local hospitality associations (regional UKHospitality chapters)

## Conversion — Trial to Paid

### Fix This First: Email Sequences

**There are currently no trial expiry emails or welcome emails implemented.** This is the highest-ROI gap to close — every trial that doesn't convert without a nudge is lost revenue.

#### Welcome Sequence (trigger: signup complete)

| Day | Subject | Content |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | *"Your trial has started — here's what to do first"* | Set up temperature logs, add your team. 3 clear steps. Link to onboarding. |
| 2 | *"Are your fridge logs up to date?"* | Show how to log a reading. Reinforce EHO value. Keep it short. |
| 4 | *"What Pro unlocks for your team"* | Soft intro to rota, timesheets, training records. Show the Pro features behind the gate. CTA: upgrade now, or wait until day 7. |
| 6 | *"Your trial ends in 24 hours"* | Urgency. Remind them their compliance records are inside Pelikn — losing access means losing that history. CTA: activate plan. |
| 7 | *"Today's the day — keep your records safe"* | Final push. Show what they've logged (X temperature records, X tasks completed). Strong CTA. Show both Starter and Pro pricing side by side. |

#### Post-Trial Lapsed (trigger: trial expired, no payment)

| Day | Subject | Content |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | *"Your records are still here"* | Reassure them their data is safe. Easy re-entry. Remove friction. |
| 7 | *"Still thinking about it?"* | Offer to answer questions. Link to FAQ. |
| 21 | *"Your 7-day free trial is waiting"* | Soft re-engagement. New angle: lead with a compliance news hook if relevant (e.g. new FSA guidance). |

## Upsell — Starter to Pro

The goal is to make the Pro upgrade feel inevitable, not like a sales push. Surface the value of Pro features at the exact moment they become relevant.

### In-App Triggers (product-led)

| Trigger | Action |
|---|---|
| User adds 5+ staff members | Show Pro rota feature: *"Ready to build your rota? Upgrade to Pro."* |
| User views the Rota section | Gate with clear upgrade prompt. Show what the AI rota builder does — don't just say "Pro only." |
| User tries to log training record | Same gate. Show the training module preview. |
| User is on Starter for 30 days | In-app banner: *"Venues on Pro save 4+ hours a week on scheduling."* |
| User opens a second venue | Prompt: *"Managing multiple venues? Pro gives you one dashboard for everything."* |

### Email Upsell Sequence (Starter users)

| Day on Starter | Trigger | Content |
|---|---|---|
| 14 | Time-based | *"How much time do you spend on your rota each week?"* — introduce AI rota builder |
| 30 | Time-based | *"Your team is growing. Here's what Pro adds."* — show timesheet + training module |
| 60 | Time-based | *"One question: are you still building rotas in Excel?"* — direct, conversational |
| On hire | Staff count crosses 10 | *"You've added 10 staff — your rota just got more complex."* |

### The Pro Value Frame

Never sell Pro as "more features." Sell it as **time saved and risk reduced**:

- *"Venues on Pro save 4+ hours a week on scheduling."*
- *"Training records with expiry alerts — know before a certificate lapses."*
- *"Clock in, clock out, payroll export. No spreadsheets."*
- *"If a staff dispute ever goes legal, you'll want a digital timesheet trail."*

The last point is underused — training records and timesheet audit trails have genuine legal value. That's a compelling reason to upgrade beyond convenience.

## Retention — Keeping Venues Sticky

Compliance data is naturally sticky. Reinforce that stickiness actively.

### Monthly Compliance Summary Email

Send every venue owner a monthly email:
- *"Your compliance summary for May"*
- X temperature checks logged
- X cleaning tasks completed
- X corrective actions resolved
- Compliance report ready to download (link)
- If Pro: X shifts rostered, X hours tracked

This email does two things: it reminds owners of the value they're getting (reducing churn) and it surfaces the Pro value to Starter users (driving upgrades).

### Training Expiry Alerts (Pro)

These are already built — make sure they're working and prominent. An alert that prevents a training certificate lapse is a moment of genuine value. Owners will remember it.

### EHO Inspection Success Stories

When a venue passes their EHO inspection (owners will often share this), reach out and ask if they'd share a testimonial or short case study. This is social proof that hits the exact emotional trigger of new prospects.

## Referral Scheme (Future)

Once there are enough happy paying venues, a referral programme is the highest-trust channel in a sector that runs on word-of-mouth:

- **Mechanic:** Refer a venue, get one month free for each that activates a paid plan
- **Positioning:** *"Tell another venue owner. They get 7 days free, you get a free month."*
- No cash payouts — account credit is simpler to administer and keeps the incentive tied to the product

The venue join code system already exists. Extend it to add a referral tracking code for owner-to-owner sharing.
