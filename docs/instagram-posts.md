## Posting Notes

**Frequency:** 3–4 posts per week to build momentum without burning out the account.

**Suggested weekly mix:**
- Monday: Compliance education (builds authority at start of week)
- Wednesday: Product spotlight or relatable content
- Friday: Comparison or call-to-action heavy post (end of week, operators thinking about next week's admin)

**Always include in caption:** Link in bio reference → keep the bio link pointed at the marketing page or a dedicated landing page.

**Story use:** Each single post can be reshared to Stories with a "swipe up" / link sticker directing to signup. Stories are where the trial CTA converts best.

**Engagement prompt:** End captions with a question where natural — "What's your current system for temperature logs?" — drives comments and boosts reach.
