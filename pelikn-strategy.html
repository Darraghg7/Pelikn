How We Retain Them Built-in gravity at every layer
1
Daily habit formation Temperature logs, opening/closing checklists, and cleaning schedules mean staff use the app every single shift. Muscle memory within a week.
2
Compliance lock-in Once you've passed an EHO inspection using Pelikn's records, switching means rebuilding your entire compliance paper trail. Nobody does that voluntarily.
3
Operational gravity (Pro) Rota + timesheets + training + time-off in one place. Switching costs compound with every week of data.
4
Multi-venue gravity A 3-site group at £55/month has all cross-venue compliance + staffing in one dashboard. Switching cost is enormous.
5
Push notifications Training certificate expiry alerts, rota changes, and time-off approvals keep the app present even when not actively in use.

Opportunities to strengthen retention further

Monthly compliance score email

Year-over-year EHO readiness trend

"You're more compliant than 6 months ago" validates the subscription cost every renewal cycle.

If staff love shift swaps and time-off requests, they become internal advocates who'd resist a switch.

Every customer who scans a table QR is a potential referral to another venue owner. Add a subtle "Get this for your venue" CTA.
