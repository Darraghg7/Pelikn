The Solution

One app. Every operation.
Zero paper.

Pelikn replaces the binder, the spreadsheet and the WhatsApp group with a single mobile-first platform purpose-built for independent hospitality.

🌡

HACCP-Grade Compliance

Fridge temps, cooking logs, hot holding, cooling records, probe calibration, cleaning schedules, delivery checks — all timestamped and audit-ready.

📅

Smart Rota & Time Tracking

AI-assisted rota builder, PIN clock-in/out, break deductions, overtime alerts, CSV timesheet export. Real-time labour cost visibility.

👥

Granular Staff Permissions

13 permission types across 3 categories. Presets for daily staff, senior staff and full access. Each team member sees only what they need.

📱

True PWA — Works Offline

Installs to homescreen. Push notifications for swaps, time-off, and late arrivals. Works in kitchens and basements with poor signal.
