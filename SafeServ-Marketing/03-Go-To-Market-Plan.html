Key Risks & Mitigations

Low staff adoption

Staff resist new tools. Paper feels easier.

Mitigation

PIN login (no email). Permission-aware dashboards. Quick actions faster than paper.

Enterprise moves downmarket

Trail or Navitas launch a “Lite” tier.

Mitigation

Enterprise DNA is hard to shed. Their UX is built for compliance managers, not baristas.

High hospitality churn

Venues close. Budgets get cut.

Mitigation

Starter at £5 resists cuts. Compliance becomes must-have for inspections. Annual billing reduces churn.
