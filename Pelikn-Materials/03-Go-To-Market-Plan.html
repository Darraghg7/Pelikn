Acquisition Channels

Six ways to reach
venue managers

A mix of inbound content, referral partnerships and targeted outbound designed to keep CAC below £45 at scale.

🔍

SEO & Content Marketing Primary

Guides on EHO prep, HACCP templates, allergen compliance. Targets high-intent searches from managers looking to digitise. Builds evergreen traffic with low marginal cost.

🤝

EHO Consultant Partnerships Primary

EHO consultants advise hundreds of venues. A white-label or referral arrangement turns each consultant into a recurring pipeline source. Revenue share model.

🎉

Referral Programme Secondary

1 month free for every venue referred. Hospitality is a tight-knit community. Word-of-mouth within local business networks has very low CAC.

🎪

Trade Shows & Events Secondary

Hospitality industry events: Pub & Bar Show, Restaurant & Bar Tech Live, Food & Drink Expo. Live demos convert high-intent visitors efficiently.

📩

Outbound Email & LinkedIn Scaling

Targeted cold outreach to venue managers and group operators. Focus on multi-site accounts (£55+/mo). Personalised by venue type and location.

📷

Social & Community Always-on

Behind-the-scenes content, EHO inspection stories, and compliance tips on Instagram and TikTok. Builds brand trust and funnels to free trial.

Phased Rollout

Three phases to
1,000 venues

Each phase has a clear goal, budget envelope and key milestone. Funding unlocks Phases 2 and 3 simultaneously.

1
Q2–Q3 2026 · 0–100 Venues

Seed — Prove the product

  • Hand-recruit 20 beta venues for free access
  • Gather qualitative feedback on onboarding and core workflows
  • Identify 3–5 highest-value feature requests
  • Activate SEO content engine and referral programme
  • Target: 100 paying venues, <5% monthly churn, NPS > 40
2
Q4 2026 · 100–300 Venues

Scale — Turn on paid acquisition

  • Launch Google Search Ads targeting EHO & compliance keywords
  • Sign first 3 EHO consultant referral partnerships
  • Hire customer success (part-time) to reduce churn
  • Introduce multi-venue group outbound motion
  • Target: 300 venues, £90k ARR, CAC < £60
3
H1 2027 · 300–1,000+ Venues

Expand — Full marketing engine

  • Trade show programme (Pub & Bar Show, Restaurant & Bar Tech Live)
  • Expand to Ireland market
  • Launch group/enterprise pricing for 5+ site operators
  • POS integrations (Square, Lightspeed, EPOS Now)
  • Target: 1,000 venues, £300k ARR, Series A pipeline

Risks & Mitigations

We know the risks.
Here’s the plan.

Four primary risks identified, each with a concrete mitigation strategy built into the GTM plan.

RiskSlow trial-to-paid conversion due to low tech adoption in independent hospitality
MitigationConcierge onboarding for first 100 venues. White-glove setup call. Reduce time-to-value below 15 minutes.
RiskLarger competitor (Trail, Hospitality Gem) responds by adding compliance features
MitigationPrice moat is durable. Offline-first architecture takes months to replicate. Move fast to lock in referral partnerships.
RiskHigh churn from Starter tier (£5/mo venues with low switching cost)
MitigationActively move Starter users to Pro within 90 days via in-app upsell. Pro users have higher stickiness due to rota dependency.
RiskCAC higher than modelled due to competitive paid search landscape
MitigationSEO and referral channels are primary at launch. Paid ads activated at Phase 2 only after organic channels are validated.
