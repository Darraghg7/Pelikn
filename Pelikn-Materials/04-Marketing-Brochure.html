The morning chef opens the Pelikn app and works through the opening checklist - fridge temps, surface hygiene, date labels checked. Each task signed with a timestamp. Manager sees it done from home before they've arrived.

A fish delivery arrives. The chef probes the crate - 3°C, within range. Logged in Pelikn in 20 seconds. If it had failed, a corrective action prompt would have appeared immediately, creating a compliant record of the decision made.
