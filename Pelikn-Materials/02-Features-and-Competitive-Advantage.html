Compliance Hub
The only module built around what an EHO inspector actually checks - not what a software developer thought they check.
Real scenario
"The inspector arrived at 10am. By 10:03 I'd handed them my phone - every temperature log, every allergen record, every corrective action for the past 90 days."
A Pelikn customer, following a 5-star FSA inspection in March 2025. The alternative: 20 minutes hunting through paper binders while the inspector waited.
01
Temperature Logging
Probe readings entered on-device with pass/fail thresholds set per product type. Failed readings trigger an immediate corrective action prompt - logged, timestamped, signed. Graphs show trends over time.
02
Natasha's Law Compliance
Full allergen matrix for every menu item - the 14 declarable allergens tracked per dish. Meets the October 2021 Natasha's Law requirements for pre-packed-for-direct-sale foods. Printable for labelling.
03
EHO Audit Simulator
A digital walkthrough of what an Environmental Health Officer checks - structured around FSA inspection criteria. Gives managers a readiness score before the real thing arrives. Flags gaps with guided remediation.
