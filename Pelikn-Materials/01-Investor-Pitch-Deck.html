PELIKN03
Why Now

Three forces converging.

1

Regulatory enforcement is tightening

The FSA’s Food Hygiene Rating Scheme is now mandatory in Wales and actively enforced across England. Natasha’s Law (allergen labelling) came into force October 2021, creating an ongoing compliance obligation every food business must meet. EHO inspection frequency is up post-pandemic. Non-compliance is costly and public.

2

Digital adoption has crossed the threshold

Post-pandemic, QR menus, contactless payment and online ordering normalised digital tools across independent hospitality. The “too complicated for our team” objection has largely evaporated. Venue managers are ready — they just haven’t found the right tool yet.

3

The competitive landscape is still fragmented

No existing player combines compliance + workforce + HR for independent venues at an accessible price. Trail addresses compliance only. Operandio is priced for enterprise. The whitespace for a £5–25/mo all-in-one is completely open.
